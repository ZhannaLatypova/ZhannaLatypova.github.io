This business cases focuses on analyzing key business metrics of advertising campaigns to evaluate their performance and optimize marketing spend.

Client
Entertainment app ProPro+ (the company name has been changed for confidentiality purposes).

The company operates in the digital entertainment industry, offering the ProPro+ mobile app.

A content and gaming platform that engages users through interactive entertainment experiences.

Business Problem
Advertising campaigns generated significant traffic but inconsistent conversions and ROI.

Despite heavy investment in online advertising (Google, Meta, influencer collaborations), the company lacked a unified understanding of which campaigns or channels delivered sustainable growth.

Marketing teams struggled to measure campaign effectiveness, identify overspending, and determine which audiences generated the highest engagement and revenue.

The absence of clear attribution led to inefficient budget allocation and missed optimization opportunities.

Goal
To evaluate and optimize advertising campaign performance using key business metrics.

The main goal was to analyze historical ad campaign data to determine which platforms, creatives, and audience segments produced the highest ROI and engagement.

The project aimed to establish a data-driven framework for decision-making, identifying underperforming campaigns, reallocating marketing budgets, and improving overall advertising efficiency.

Data
The dataset included aggregated campaign performance metrics.
  • Campaign name, date, platform (Google, Meta, TikTok)
  • Impressions, Clicks, CTR, CPC, CPA, CPM
  • Conversions and revenue metrics
  • Audience demographic data
  • Budget and spend data

    • Source: Company’s marketing analytics system (ProPro+ internal dashboard).

    Data was collected via APIs and exported to CSV for analysis.

Models Used / Approach
Rather than predictive modeling, this project used an analytical and statistical approach
  • Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
  • Correlation analysis between cost and conversion metrics
  • ROI, ROAS, and engagement metric calculations
  • Cohort and segmentation analysis
  • Visualization of performance trends (CTR, CPC, CPA, ROI)
  • Identification of top-performing campaigns
Results
Two evaluation metrics
  • Identified top 3 campaigns with ROI > 180% and CTR above 12%.
  • Found that Platform A (Meta Ads) delivered 2.5x higher conversions per dollar than TikTok.
  • Discovered high CPC inefficiencies in one ad group (−25% ROAS).
  • Recommended reallocation of 15% of total ad spend to high-performing segments, projected to increase total ROI by 22%.
Tools
Python libraries for analysis
  • Python (pandas, numpy, matplotlib, seaborn, plotly
  • Scikit-learn (metrics)
  • Jupyter Notebook
  • Excel / Power BI for final dashboards
Business Impact
Better decision-making
  • Enhanced visibility into ad performance across channels.
  • Enabled data-driven budget reallocation, improving ROI and reducing wasted ad spend.
  • Supported marketing team decisions with quantifiable metrics and visual dashboards.
  • Projected 22% increase in advertising efficiency and improved audience targeting accuracy.
