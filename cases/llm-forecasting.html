CASE STUDY

LLM + Time Series Forecasting for E-commerce

Goal: improve demand forecasting combining classic time-series models with LLM-based enrichment (events, promos, seasonality notes).

Context

Business Problem

Stockouts and overstock increased costs & lost revenue. Needed a more robust weekly forecast at SKU × region.

Approach

Data & Method

  • Historical sales, price, promo flags, holidays
  • LLM to turn unstructured notes → structured regressors
  • Baseline: Prophet / XGBoost; Hybrid: features + LLM
Results

Impact

MAPE ↓ 12–18% vs baseline; safety stock optimized; stockouts ↓ ~9%, holding costs ↓ ~7% in pilot categories.

Role

My contribution

Problem framing, data pipeline, modeling, evaluation, dashboards, rollout plan.
