A bit more about me ...

I uncover the stories hidden within data and transform them into actionable solutions.

My journey didn't begin with numbers alone—it started with curiosity. I've always believed that behind every dataset lies a meaningful story waiting to be told. As a Data Scientist, my mission is to act as a translator, turning complex algorithms into clear business strategies.
